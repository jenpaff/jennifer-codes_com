About Jennifer

Jennifer currently works as a Senior Software Developer at ThoughtWorks. She graduated in 2016 with a degree in Advanced Computer Science from The University of Sheffield, UK. Having started her career working with Microservices in a Java/Spring Boot environment, she's had the chance of working in the smart ecosystems/iOT space and is keen to branch out to emerging technologies such as blockchain tech, following her passion to solve hard technical challenges in a constraint environment.

As a Systers Keeper- leaders of an international women in tech community- she's a passionate advocate for Diversity in Tech and is convinced that diverse teams build better products.
